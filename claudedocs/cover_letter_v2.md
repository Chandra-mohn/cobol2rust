Dear [CXO Name],

I am writing to propose a strategic partnership between [Company Name] and a
new venture I am founding, focused on building an AI-accelerated enterprise
engineering platform -- a suite of products that combines the speed and
intelligence of AI with the reliability and auditability of deterministic
code generation.

Over my tenure at [Company Name], I have seen the same pattern repeat across
every major engineering initiative: brilliant engineers spend months writing
code that follows well-known patterns, while the real intellectual work --
understanding requirements, making architectural decisions, defining business
rules -- happens in meetings and spreadsheets that never make it into the
codebase. The knowledge lives in people's heads. The code is just the
mechanical output.

This observation led me to build Nexflow -- a platform your leadership team
has seen firsthand and recognized for its power. Nexflow proved that when you
capture intent in a domain-specific language and let a deterministic compiler
generate the code, you get something remarkable: AI can help create the DSL
at unprecedented speed, domain experts can review it because it speaks their
language, and the compiler guarantees that the output is correct, auditable,
and identical every time.

**That pattern -- AI proposes, humans decide, compilers deliver -- is
the foundation of everything I am building.**

The Nexflow model extends far beyond stream processing. I have built and am
building products that apply this same architecture across the entire
enterprise engineering lifecycle:

- **Nexflow** -- DSL-to-code generation, expanding from stream processing
  into APIs, microservices, NACHA/ACH processing, banking transactions,
  and more
- **cobol2rust** -- AI-assisted COBOL analysis paired with deterministic
  Rust transpilation (901 tests passing, 46/47 stress tests validated)
- **coqu** -- AI-powered natural language interface for legacy code
  comprehension
- **ArchStudio** -- AI-driven architecture design with deterministic
  infrastructure-as-code generation
- **DVF** -- AI-assisted data comparison analysis with deterministic
  validation pipelines
- And additional domain-specific products in development

The common thread: AI makes engineers faster. Deterministic compilation makes
the output trustworthy. Domain-specific languages make the intent reviewable
by subject-matter experts who are not programmers.

**I am proposing that [Company Name] become the founding strategic partner of
this venture.** Specifically:

1. **Preferred Licensing** -- Enterprise access at founding-partner rates,
   well below market pricing, locked for three years
2. **Co-Development Influence** -- A seat at the product advisory table,
   ensuring the roadmap addresses [Company Name]'s real-world needs first
3. **Equity Participation** -- An optional minority equity stake at pre-seed
   valuation, giving [Company Name] financial upside as the platform scales
4. **AI + Domain Expertise Synergy** -- Your domain experts and our AI-driven
   tools, working together to define the DSLs that encode your business
   knowledge into reproducible, auditable systems

The products already exist. The technology is proven. Nexflow alone
demonstrated what is possible. Now I am ready to scale this approach across
every domain where enterprises write repetitive, pattern-driven code -- which
is to say, nearly everywhere.

The enclosed presentation details the full vision, product portfolio,
